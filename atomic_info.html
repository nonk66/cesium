Cesium Atomic Information

Symbol: Cs

Atomic number: 55

Ion charge: +

Atomic mass: 132.91

Common isotopes: Cesium has the second most known isotopes of all elements, but the only naturally occuring one is Cesium-133.

Relative abundance: Cesium is relatively rare, being the 45th most abundant element and the 36th most abundant metal. It averages ~3 parts per million in the Earth's crust.
